About Me

Describing oneself is one of the most difficult tasks in the world. However, I am going to describe myself in three different sections; first of all I would like to draw your attention on my personality because I believe personality is the most important phenomenon that helps a person to build a relationship with others. Then I will focus on my professional and academic expertise.
Luckily, I am obsessed with staying organized and making everyone’s life easier. It brings such a genuine joy to my heart to take the burden off of my loved one’s shoulders, and I always do anything I can to enhance that. Work is no different. There are not many things that can grow a company the way that trust and dependability can, and I pride myself in being so dedicated to both.
I have very diversified professional and academic backgrounds. I worked as a newspaper deliverer, car builder, sales person, shop manager, medical promotion executive for pharmaceutical companies, and teacher in a high school. In addition, I was an activist for Greenpeace Finland. Moreover, my mom owns a social welfare organization that works for distressed women in Bangladesh; so I have some practical experiences on development projects that aimed to make distressed women’s life better.
Last but not least, I studied several faculties that include General Science, Accounting, English Literature, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, Intercultural Communication, and Public Management. Currently I am studying Full Stack Web Development at Business College Helsinki. I have two master’s degrees; one in Sociology and other one in Public Management. The performances throughout my student life are outstanding. For example, my average grade in second master’s degree in Public Management is 4 out of 5. My second master’s degree makes me specialist for multinational companies where the work force and communications are multicultural and diverse. The aim of the degree is to make experts for intercultural communication in multinational companies.
